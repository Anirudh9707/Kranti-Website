about kranti2K23

National Level Technical Symposium

Kranti 2023 is a national level technical Symposium organised by the department of Computer Science and Engineering, Meenakshi Sundararajan Engineering College. This event brings together some of the brightest minds in the technical field to share their knowledge, collaborate with each other and hone their skills. Our symposium features a diverse range of technical topics- spanning across a number of events. The lineup includes invigorating events like Algolia, TechTonic, and Shark tank. Technology has undergone remarkable advancements over the past few decades. In the early years, computers were massive and expensive machines that were accessible only to large organisations. Today, technology is more accessible and affordable to everyone. Our theme of taking a leap across time promises you a journey from where it all began to where we are today. Register soon and don’t miss out on this magnificent time trek.
